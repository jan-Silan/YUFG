Our Story

Youth United for Good was founded by Eythan Soysa and Siddhant Mathur, with a shared desire to make the world a better place through meaningful action. The charity was created to harness the creativity and compassion of youth who wanted to make a tangible difference. We saw an opportunity to focus on supporting charities that deliver the most effective aid per unit spent. And so, Youth United for Good began its journey, building a movement of young people driven by purpose, ready to change the world one fundraiser at a time.

Current Outreach

We have a rapidly growing network of over 50 students in more than 40 partner schools in the UK, UAE, Qatar, Oman, and Pakistan.
Previous schools we have worked with include St Olave’s Grammar School, Altrincham Grammar School for Boys, and Wychwood school, raising over £1600 in the process.
